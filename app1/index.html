Poovan Pazham Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Poovan Pazham is a free-to-play game developed for entertainment purposes. Your privacy is important to us, and this app is designed to respect it fully.

Information Collection

Poovan Pazham does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No login or registration required
  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or identifiers collected
  • No location data accessed
  • No analytics or tracking tools used

Advertising

The app displays AdMob banner advertisements at the bottom of the screen.

AdMob may collect non-personal data (such as device type or ad interactions) as per Google's advertising policies. This data collection is handled entirely by Google and not by the app developer.

You can review Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

Since Poovan Pazham does not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data misuse or leakage from the app.

Children's Privacy

Poovan Pazham does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The game is safe for all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected within the app or on the app store listing.
